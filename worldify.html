Open live editor

Overview

Worldify is a browser-based map editor that lets you compose game worlds tile by tile. The goal was a tool that feels immediate — place, paint, and edit without friction — while staying powerful enough for larger maps.

I focused on the editing experience: a clear canvas, a tidy tool panel, and interactions that stay out of the way so the map is always the centre of attention.
