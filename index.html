I’m an Argentinian–Italian UX/UI Designer with over three years of experience, focused on building scalable design systems and intuitive interfaces for digital products. I’m currently based in Argentina, collaborating remotely with teams around the world.

My path into design actually began with front-end development. At first, I was fascinated by code, but I found myself spending more and more time refining how things looked and felt to use. That curiosity grew into a passion for interface design and, eventually, for creating complete design systems.

Living and working in different countries — including Germany — gave me a deeper appreciation for how culture shapes design. I speak English, Spanish, and German, and I’m always looking for new challenges that connect creativity, technology, and purpose.
